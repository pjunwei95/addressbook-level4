About Us

We are a team consisting of four members based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
We are currently Computer Science students pursuing an undergraduate degree here at NUS. Our TeamID is W13-B2.

The Weaver application was developed by our team for our Software Engineering project. It is adapted from the AddressBook - Level 4 that was developed by the se-edu team.
We have renamed the application as such so that it evokes a positive appeal among others.
Weaver symbolises the weaving of out contacts in order to stay connected.
